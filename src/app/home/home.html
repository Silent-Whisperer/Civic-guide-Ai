Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common voting questions.

What documents do I need to register to vote? expand_more
You need a recent passport-sized photograph, an age proof (like a Birth Certificate, PAN Card, or 10th marksheet), and an address proof (like an Aadhaar Card, Passport, or recent utility bill).
Can I vote if I don't have my Voter ID (EPIC) card on election day? expand_more
Yes! As long as your name is on the electoral roll (voter list), you can vote by showing an alternative approved ID, such as your Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, or Passport.
How do I check if my name is on the voter list? expand_more
You can check your name on the official ECI portal (electoralsearch.eci.gov.in) by searching with your details (name, age, state) or by using your EPIC (Voter ID) number.
What is an EVM and how do I use it? expand_more
An EVM (Electronic Voting Machine) is the device used to record votes. To vote, simply press the blue button next to the symbol/name of the candidate you choose. A red light will glow, and you will hear a beep confirming your vote.
